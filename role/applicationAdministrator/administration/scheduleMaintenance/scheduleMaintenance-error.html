Schedule maintenance

Attention

  1. Please enter the maintenance date.
  2. The maintenance date entered may not occur in the past.
  3. The maintenance date entered must be in the YYYY-MM-DD format.
  4. Please select the maintenance time.
  5. Please select the maintenance duration.

To schedule a maintenance period, indicate the date, time and duration below. A notification will be displayed to users starting 48 hours in advance of the maintenance period, and will disable all services 2 hours in advance to prevent the interruption of exams in progress or payments in progress. When the maintenance period begins, all users will be logged off and redirected accordingly.
